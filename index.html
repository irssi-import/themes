Irssi Themes

If you want your themes here, mail them to themes@irssi.org. Screenshots in PNG or GIF formats only. No jpegs please, they just look worse and take a lot more space.

Usage
Copy the .theme file to ~/.irssi/ directory, change the theme with /SET theme theme_name
Why doesn't it look like the screenshot?
Screenshots are user-contributed. They contain a lot of information which is not part of the theme file:
  • Background colour and image
  • font face and base colour
  • Additional scripts such as: nicklist, window list, nick colouring script.
In most cases, the authors only sent in the theme files and none of those additional material.
Why is my irssi in a foreign language?
Several themes have translated all irssi messages into the author's language. You can either edit those themes yourself or avoid them :-)
